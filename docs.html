üéÆ Game Calculator

Overview

The Game Calculator is tailored for gaming analytics: weapon statistics, reload times, damage calculations, and time-to-kill (TTK) computations. Perfect for FPS and tactical shooter enthusiasts.

RPM & Rate of Fire Converter

Convert between rotational and firearm rate-of-fire measurements.

Conversion Types

  • Standard RPM: Rotations per minute (mechanical RPM)
  • Firearm ROF: Rounds per minute (bullets fired)

Available Units

Standard RPM: RPM, RPS, Hz, rad/s

Firearm ROF: RPM, RPS, RPH

Example: 600 RPM firearm ROF = 10 RPS (rounds per second)

Reload Time Calculator

Analyze the time cycle of a weapon including firing and reloading.

Inputs

  • Magazine Size: Number of rounds per magazine
  • Reload Time: Time in seconds to reload
  • Rounds Per Second (RPS): Fire rate in shots per second

Outputs

  • Time to Fire Magazine: How long to empty one magazine
  • Total Cycle Time: Reload + fire time
  • DPS (Magazine Cycle): Damage per second across the cycle

Weapon Swap & Rotation Time

Calculate the total time to rotate between two weapons.

Inputs

  • Weapon 1 Reload Time: Reload time for first weapon
  • Weapon 2 Reload Time: Reload time for second weapon
  • Swap Time: Time to switch between weapons

Output

Total Rotation Time: Complete reload cycle for both weapons

Use Case: Optimize your weapon rotation strategy for sustained damage.

DPS Calculator

Calculate burst and sustained damage per second with accuracy factored in.

Inputs

  • Damage Per Shot: Base damage per round
  • Fire Rate (RPM): Rounds per minute
  • Magazine Size: Rounds per mag
  • Reload Time: Reload duration
  • Accuracy (%): Hit percentage (0-100%)

Outputs

  • Burst DPS: Damage output during continuous firing
  • Sustain DPS: Average DPS including reload time
  • Magazine Damage: Total damage per magazine (with accuracy)
  • Fire Rate: Shots per second

TTK (Time To Kill) Calculator

Determine how many shots and how long to eliminate a target.

Inputs

  • Target Health: Enemy health pool
  • Damage Per Shot: Base damage
  • Fire Rate (RPM): Shots per minute
  • Headshot Multiplier: Damage multiplier for headshots (e.g., 2.0x)

Outputs

  • Shots to Kill (Body): Body shot count needed
  • TTK (Body): Time to kill with body shots
  • Shots to Kill (Headshot): Headshot count needed
  • TTK (Headshot): Time to kill with headshots
Pro Tip: Compare body vs. headshot TTK to evaluate the importance of accuracy in your weapon selection.

Gaming Calculator Tips

  • Use accurate game statistics from in-game weapons or official wikis
  • TTK is calculated from shot-to-shot delay (not server tick rate)
  • Accuracy affects real-world DPS; use realistic percentages for sustained fire
  • Magazine cycling affects sustain DPS significantly ‚Äî fast reloads matter!

‚ùì FAQ

General Questions

Is CC App free?

Yes! CC App is completely free to use with no ads or paid features.

Do I need an account?

No account required. All settings are saved locally in your browser.

Can I use CC App offline?

Most features work offline once loaded. The graphing and game calculation features require server-side computation, so they need internet access.

How accurate are the conversions?

All conversions use standard conversion factors and are accurate to at least 4-6 decimal places. Precision can be adjusted in Settings.

Calculator Questions

Why does my calculation show "Error"?

Common causes: division by zero, invalid syntax, or unsupported operations. Check your expression and try again.

What's the difference between sin and sinh?

sin(x): Regular sine function. sinh(x): Hyperbolic sine function (e^x - e^-x)/2.

How do I use radians instead of degrees?

All trig functions in scientific calculator use radians. To convert degrees to radians: multiply by œÄ/180.

Can I use parentheses in expressions?

Yes! Use parentheses to control order of operations: (2+3)*4 = 20, not 14.

Graphing Questions

My graph looks wrong or shows no points. What's wrong?

Common issues:

  • Check syntax: use * for multiplication (not implicit), ^ for exponents
  • Verify function is defined in the x-range you specified
  • Some functions have asymptotes or discontinuities (like 1/x)
  • Adjust y-range manually if Auto Y doesn't work

Why does the graph look pixelated or jagged?

Increase resolution in Settings to get a smoother curve. High resolution = more computation but smoother display.

Game Calculator Questions

How do I find my weapon's exact damage and RPM?

Check the game's wiki, in-game descriptions, or community resources. Some games display this in the weapon menu or inspection screen.

Does accuracy affect TTK?

TTK is based on number of shots, not accuracy. Accuracy affects DPS but not the raw TTK calculation. For realistic comparisons, adjust your accuracy percentage in DPS calculator.

What does "sustain DPS" mean?

Sustain DPS accounts for reload time and magazine size, showing real-world average damage over an extended fight. Burst DPS is damage during continuous firing without reloads.

Settings Questions

Will my settings sync across devices?

No, settings are stored locally in your browser. If you use a different device or browser, you'll need to configure settings again.

Can I export my settings?

Not yet. Settings are stored in browser localStorage. Future updates may add export/import functionality.

Common Issues & Solutions

‚ùå "Invalid input values" error

Problem: You entered non-numeric data or left fields empty.

Solution: Ensure all required fields contain numbers. Remove any letters, symbols (except decimals), or spaces.

‚ùå Converter shows no results

Solution: Verify the category and unit dropdowns are set correctly. Click "Convert" again.

‚ùå Calculator displays "Error"

‚ùå Graph won't plot

Solution:

  • Try a different x-range
  • Adjust y-range manually if Auto Y fails

‚ùå Settings not saving

Problem: Browser localStorage is disabled or full.

Solution:

  • Check browser privacy settings allow localStorage
  • Clear browser cache/storage if full
  • Try a different browser
  • Make sure you clicked "Save Settings"

‚ùå Theme not applying

Problem: CSS not loading or cache issue.

Solution:

  • Hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try a different browser

‚ùå Graphing is slow or unresponsive

Problem: Resolution set too high or complex expression.

Solution:

  • Lower resolution in Settings (use "Low")
  • Reduce x-range to smaller interval
  • Simplify your expression
  • Try in a different browser or device
